Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers! Our FAQs section provides clarity on how the platform works, the services we offer, and how you can make the most out of your financial advisory experience. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out to us!

1. How do I get started with the platform?

Simply fill out a few questions about your financial goals and preferences. This will help us generate a personalized report for you.

2. How accurate are the investment strategies provided?

Our AI-powered model analyzes your profile and provides strategies based on industry standards and market trends. However, it’s important to remember that investing involves risk, and we recommend cross-referencing with a professional financial advisor.

3. Will I get updates on the stock market daily?

Yes! You’ll receive daily market updates, including key trends, performance summaries, and stock highlights, ensuring you stay informed.

4. Can I view my report in different formats?

Absolutely! You can view a full detailed report or a quick outline version for a fast overview of the recommendations.

5. How do I receive stock updates via WhatsApp?

Once you sign up, simply link your WhatsApp number to your account, and we’ll send you real-time stock trends and market updates directly.

6. Is my data safe on this platform?

Yes, your data is stored securely with the highest privacy standards. We ensure that your personal and financial information is never shared without your consent.
